Welcome to FAIRship!

The idea is FAIR sharing of algorithm. Suppose a researcher wants to test their hypothesis on among patients who diagnosed with Cardiovascular disease (CVD) young women has lower blood glucose levels than young men.

An algorithm designed and implemented to test this hypothesis and shipped to Data Integration Centers (DICs) to train
  • Data is searched in centralized schema repository
  • Consumer describes algorithm (metadata, data requirements, other requirements like minimum data or reputation threshold)
  • Algorithm placed onto train and intermediary results are sent along an optimal path
  • Result is sent back to Consumer
